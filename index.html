Welcome To CPPM

CPPM, is a non-government, non-profitable, non-political, voluntary and charitable organization, It will perform its activities, to the best of its efforts, in the interest of the mass people regardless of race, religion, color and nation and it has been working from ten years to help, to serve and to improve the educational, social and economic condition of our beloved country. CPPM is working by several projects to support the needy people with the commitment of â€œserving humanity in all over the country. and provides relief to the victims of natural disasters, CPPM also deploys long-term development projects on education, skills training, Orphan Care, Community Services, provision of clean water and healthcare to tackle the root causes of poverty Its purpose is to develop innovative and sustainable solutions that enable individuals as well as communities to live with dignity and by supporting initiatives that promote educational standards and decrease educational disparity in the society. The vision of CPPM is ...


  • alleviation of poverty, discrimination and all kind of injustices form society, education for all and provision of basic amenities for those in needs in order to create a welfare society.

  • CPPM is registered organization with the Joint Stock Companies & Firms under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, Government of Bangladesh.

  • CPPM started its operation on 1st January 2008 The foundation came into existence through the initiative of a group of scholars, educationists and academicians to undertake programs for harmonization of education, culture, morality and ethics.

  • It has come forward with a vision of making a generation capable of and ideal for the nation and for the humanity at large.
